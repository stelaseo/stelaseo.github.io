I am a Ph.D. student in Human-Robot Interaction Lab at the University of Manitoba. I am interested in multi-robot teleoperation, social robotics, interactive contents design, and many others.

Visual Team State Indicators (2020)

We explored various widgets to show team robots' working states in a teleoperation interface.



In-feed embedded techniques for visualizing robot team member locations (2017)

We presented a set of mini-map alternatives for indicating the relative locations of robot team members in a teleoperation interface.

Interactive Detail-in-Context Using Two Pan-and-Tilt Cameras to Improve Teleoperation Effectiveness (2017)

We explored how we can integrate two steerable cameras to improve teleoperation task effectiveness.

Picassnake: the painting robot (2017)

Picassnake generated an unique abstract painting from music.



Women and Men Collaborating with Robots on Assembly Lines (2015)

We presented an original scenario design specifically created for exploring gender-related issues surrounding collaborative human-robot teams on assembly lines.

A Simulated Robot versus a Real Robot: People's Empathic Response (2015)

Do simulated robots provide authentic interaction as a real robot does in terms of people's empathic responses?

Alerting Users by Animating Content on a Transforming Tabletop Interface (2014)

We explored animations to convey a transformable table robot's intention to people around it.

A Touchscreen Interface for Controlling a Robotic Arm (2013)

A touchscreen interface for the remote control of a multi-degree of freedom robot arm to reduce task load and fatigue.

Exploring the Role of Affect Recognition in Web-Capable Applications (2012)

We explored people's opinions and reactions to the possibility of emotion-aware adaptive web user interfaces.

MotionController (2011)

An utility for humanoid robot to generate motions by interpolating points of end-effectors with parameters of curve shape.
